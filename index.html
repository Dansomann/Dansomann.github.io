About the school

Nimbus Heights High School was founded in the early 1920s by a group of visionary educators and community leaders who sought to provide the children of Nimbus with a well-rounded education. The school was initially a modest three-story building, accommodating a small student population. As the town grew and prospered, so did the school.
Our school provides high end technology, football(soccer), basketball, volleyball, cross country running, sailing, rowing, swimming, and water skiing.
